Key Figures of the American Civil War

Abraham Lincoln

16th President of the United States who led the nation through the Civil War and worked to end slavery with the Emancipation Proclamation, issued in 1863.

Ulysses S. Grant

Leading Union general who later became the 18th President of the United States, known for his military strategy and leadership.

Robert E. Lee

Commander of the Confederate Army known for his tactical brilliance, particularly in battles such as Gettysburg and Antietam.

William T. Sherman

Union general famous for his "March to the Sea," which aimed to cripple the South's war effort through total war tactics.

Edwin Stanton

Secretary of War under Lincoln who played a crucial role in managing the war effort and overseeing military operations.
